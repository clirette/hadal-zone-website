Hadal Zone

Hadal Zone is a fast-paced, Shoot 'Em up game inspired in part by the "Aquas" level in StarFox 64. Take control of a submarine in the battle to stop the Abbysal Cultists from corrupting marine creatures, and from summoning a great and ancient evil from the depths of the ocean. Unlock dozens of parts for your submarine, and assemble your perfect, cultist-killing aquatic machine!
